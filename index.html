A Junk Material Bidding System

A household app to find any kind of marketable waste materials that can be offered through on line ,using the app will help you recycle your marketable waste items into money.

Application Features

Simple

The App is user-friendly it is easy to navigate and simple to use just use the camera of you phone capture the image and wait until the app determines the material if it's salable or not.

Customize

You can also customize the information about the material you are selling online, You can also add details about the item to gain more bidders.

Security

The Admin makes sure's that you are protected and secure from bogus bidders we require junk shops to capture their business permits to make sure that they are the real deal and not bogus bidders or buyers.
